What is Thai Dry Massage?

Thai Dry Massage is an ancient healing system that combines acupressure, Indian Ayurvedic principles, and assisted yoga postures. Unlike other forms of massage, Thai Dry Massage is performed on a mat on the floor with the recipient wearing loose, comfortable clothing.

This unique form of bodywork focuses on energy lines and pressure points throughout the body to release blockages and tension, improve flexibility, and balance the body's energy systems.

Benefits of Thai Dry Massage

  • Boosts energy levels and circulation
  • Increases flexibility and range of motion
  • Reduces stress and promotes relaxation
  • Relieves muscle tension and pain
  • Improves posture and body alignment
  • Enhances mental clarity and focus
